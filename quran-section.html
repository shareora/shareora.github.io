My Al-Qur'an Learning Journey

Bismillahirahmanirahiim.
بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيْم

Bismillah 🌸

Welcome, dear soul.

This section is dedicated to learning and reflecting on the most beautiful and complete guide — the Al-Qur’an.

Whether you're reading it for the first time or returning to it with a new heart, know that every step you take toward the Qur’an is a step toward light, healing, and closeness to Allah.

You don’t have to know everything. You don’t have to be perfect. You just need to begin — with sincerity.

Let’s walk this journey together, slowly and meaningfully, insyaAllah.

So we gonna start with reciting Al-Fatehah before we go further.

📖 Surah Al-Fatihah

بِسْمِ ٱللَّهِ ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

Bismillāhi r-raḥmāni r-raḥīm

In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.

ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَٰلَمِينَ

Al-ḥamdu lillāhi rabbil-‘ālamīn

All praise is due to Allah, Lord of the worlds.

ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

Ar-raḥmāni r-raḥīm

The Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.

مَـٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

Māliki yawmi d-dīn

Master of the Day of Judgment.

إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

Iyyāka na‘budu wa iyyāka nasta‘īn

You alone we worship, and You alone we ask for help.

ٱهْدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ

Ihdinā ṣ-ṣirāṭ al-mustaqīm

Guide us to the straight path.

صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ غَيْرِ ٱلْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ

Ṣirāṭ al-ladhīna an‘amta ‘alayhim ghayril-maghḍūbi ‘alayhim walā ḍ-ḍāllīn

The path of those You have blessed — not of those who earned Your anger, nor of those who went astray.

Amiin. May Allah guide us all. InsyaAllah.

Let us start with our journey.
